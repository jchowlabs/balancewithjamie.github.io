Finding Balance While
Living With Lupus.

Hi, I'm Jamie. Living with lupus has shown me how hard it can be to find balance and care for yourself. As a registered nurse and health coach, I blend professional knowledge with personal experience to help you feel empowered, manage symptoms, and bring more calm into daily life.

True healing starts from within.

It's more than just medication - it's about nurturing your body with intention each day. Healing means looking beyond symptoms to understand the root cause, and building a lifestyle that nourishes your body, mind, and spirit.

Health is built on Four Pillars.

Food

Food is medicine, providing your body with the right nutrients it needs to heal.

Sleep

Quality sleep is essential for restoring and repairing your body.

Stress Management

Managing stress helps reduce inflammation and supports your overall well-being.

Movement

Movement - whether gentle or active - helps keep your body resilient and strong.

When these four pillars are in BALANCE, your body has the best chance to thrive.
